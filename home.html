Troy Alabama

Troy is a city in Pike County, Alabama, United States. As of the 2010 census, the population was 18,033. The city is the county seat of Pike County. Troy is a college town and is home to Troy University. The city has a total area of 26.3 square miles, of which, 26.2 square miles of it is land and 0.1 square miles is water.

Troy University

It was founded on February 26, 1887 as Troy State Normal School within the Alabama State University System. It is the flagship university of the Troy University System with its main campus enrollment of 6,998 students and the total enrollment of all Troy University campuses of 19,579.
